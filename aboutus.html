Ruwe Kopi ☆

Born from a love of coffee and a deep appreciation for culture, Ruwe Kopi blends the warmth of Kelantanese tradition with the inspiration of Indonesia’s coffee heritage. The name "Ruwe" is pronounced ru (as in "ruby") and we (as in "where"), a playful nod to its unique identity.

Our journey began in December 2022 as a humble coffee vendor in Tanah Merah. By March 2023, fueled by passion and dedication, we opened our first physical shop. Starting with just six chairs and three tables, Ruwe Kopi has grown into a cozy haven for coffee lovers—a place to gather, connect, and savor every sip.

Inspired by a soul-refreshing trip across Indonesia, Miss Anonymous founded Ruwe Kopi, transforming her passion for coffee into her first-ever business. The idea was to establish a "aesthetically pleasing" coffee shop that doubles as a community in addition to a café.

We invite you to join us and experience the perfect blend of culture, coffee, and connection.

BEWARE OF COFFEE BUMP!

Opening Hours: Everyday from 1 P.M. to 1 A.M.

Address: Beside Kak Mah Satay, Machang.
